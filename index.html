KNOWLEDGE BASE

Frequently Asked Questions

What steel products do you manufacture and export?

We manufacture and export a comprehensive range of premium steel products including Carbon Steel Billets, Deformed Rebar, Continuous-Cast Steel Slabs, Steel Sheets, Structural H-Beams, Wire Rods, and Seamless Steel Pipes for the international steel market.

Are you a certified Iran Steel Exporter?

Yes, Mohajer Mobin Steel is an enterprise-level professional steel company and leading supplier based in Iran. We manage complex steel logistics, CIF/FOB shipping, and export documentation to deliver to global destinations like the UAE, Turkey, Germany, Russia, and the broader Middle East, European, and Asian steel markets.

What international steel standards do your products meet?

Our industrial steel and construction materials are rigorously tested and certified to meet stringent international steel standards including ASTM, DIN, EN Standards, and ISO, ensuring top-tier engineering materials for our global clients.

How do you ensure quality control and inspection?

We implement rigorous metallurgical quality control protocols at every stage of steel processing and production. Our advanced laboratory testing and third-party inspection (such as SGS) ensure that all carbon steel and alloy steel products possess the required tensile strength, yield strength, durability, and precise chemical composition before warehouse dispatch.

How are your steel products packaged and shipped?

Our supply chain team ensures secure, export-grade packaging tailored to each product type, preventing corrosion and transit damage. We handle all logistics, from warehouse loading to port delivery, offering flexible CIF and FOB shipping options for our international trade partners.

Do you provide Mill Test Certificates (MTC)?

Yes, comprehensive Mill Test Certificates (MTC) detailing the chemical composition and mechanical properties are provided with every shipment to guarantee transparency and compliance with engineering specifications.

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

Our Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) depends on the specific steel product, manufacturing requirements, and shipping method (break-bulk or containerized). Please contact our B2B sales team for precise procurement details.

Do you provide technical support for international customers?

Absolutely. Our dedicated customer support and engineering teams work closely with international procurement managers to provide technical specifications, material data sheets, and custom manufacturing solutions tailored to specific industrial requirements.
